About Us

Our history

One-Life Org was founded in 2010 by Sun Ho Donovan, now the director, and Andrew Owen, the current CEO. What began as a small, heartfelt initiative to help the less privileged has grown into a global organization making a real difference in people’s lives. From the very start, the focus was clear: provide education for children, support vulnerable communities, and improve the world quietly but effectively.

Over the years, One-Life Org has reached more than 1.28 million people across four continents and eight countries. The organization prides itself on working without seeking recognition or awards, believing that true impact is measured by the lives changed, not by public accolades. Many of its supporters and donors choose to remain anonymous, including influential figures and celebrities, because the mission is about giving, not fame.

Beyond education, One-Life Org is committed to environmental sustainability. Through programs like tree planting and community development projects, the organization actively works to create healthier, greener, and more resilient communities. Every action, no matter how small, is aimed at leaving a positive, lasting impact.

Humility is at the heart of One-Life Org. Its team works silently, driven by the belief that helping others and protecting the planet is a responsibility, not a choice. Today, One-Life Org continues to grow, inspire, and transform lives, proving that quiet dedication can create extraordinary change.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities and individuals to overcome challenges and thrive. We focus on sustainable solutions that address the root causes of issues, ensuring long-term positive change.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has the opportunity to live a healthy, fulfilling life, free from poverty and injustice. We strive to create a more equitable and compassionate global community.
